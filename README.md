# 👻 Third Settler

**The player who always shows up.**

Third Settler is a free web app that plays the *third player* — so two people can finally
enjoy the board games that normally need three or four.

---

## The problem

Some of the best board games on the shelf quietly demand three or four players. If it's
just you and your kid on a Tuesday night, that shelf may as well be locked. Single parents
hit this wall constantly: the game is *right there*, and you still can't play it.

## The promise

Third Settler runs the missing player for you — the **Ghost**. You play on the real board
you already own, with the real pieces. The app generates the map, rolls the dice, takes the
Ghost's turns, and keeps score. Two humans, one friendly phantom, a real game night.

No login. No cost. Works on any phone. Built to keep your eyes on your kid and the table —
not on a screen.

## What it is (and isn't)

- ✅ A **companion / game master** for board games you already own.
- ✅ Generic by design — it works *alongside* your physical box.
- ❌ Not a copy of any game. No trademarked names, logos, artwork, or rulebook text.

## The story

Built by a dad and his almost-8-year-old son who got tired of not being able to play their
favorite hex-and-resource game with just the two of them. If it helps one other single
parent have a better game night, it did its job.
