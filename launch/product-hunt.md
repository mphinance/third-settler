# Product Hunt launch kit — DRAFT

## Name
Third Settler

## Tagline (60-char max)
**Play 3-4 player board games with just two of you**

Alternates:
- The third player who always shows up
- A friendly ghost plays the empty chair at game night

## Description (~260-char max)
Some of the best board games need 3-4 players. Third Settler runs the missing
one. It deals a balanced board, rolls the dice, plays a phantom third player
(the Ghost), and tracks score, so two people can finally play. Free, no login,
works offline.

## First comment (from the maker)

Hi Product Hunt 👋

I build algo-trading tools for a living. This one is different.

It is a Tuesday and it is just me and my almost-eight-year-old. We want to play
the big hex-and-resource board game on the shelf. It needs three or four
players. We are two. Back on the shelf it goes.

Single parents know this shelf well. So my son and I built the missing player
instead. We call it the Ghost.

Third Settler is a companion app, not a video game. You play on the real board
you already own. The app deals you a balanced map, rolls the dice, takes the
Ghost's turns, and keeps score. The Ghost is a friendly blocker that hogs the
good spots and never argues about whose turn it is.

It is free, has no accounts, works offline, and is open source. If it gives one
more single parent a better game night, it did its job.

Would love your feedback, and your worst dad jokes about ghosts.
