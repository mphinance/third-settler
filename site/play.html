How two-player works

The quick reference. You can close this and come back any time.

Turn order

You, then your opponent, then the Ghost. Repeat until someone hits 10.

The Ghost 👻

  • 4 blocker settlements were placed at setup. They never produce and never score.
  • On the Ghost's turn it rolls (everyone collects), then builds one road — the app tells you roughly where.

Dice & the robber

  • Whoever's turn it is rolls; everyone collects from matching hexes.
  • Roll a 7: move the robber and anyone holding 8+ cards discards half.

Trading & winning

  • House rule: bank trades are 3-for-1. Ports work as printed.
  • First player to 10 points wins.
